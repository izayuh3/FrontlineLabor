Event labor you can count on

General Stagehands

Decking, risers, truss assist, cabling, scenic setup, and general show support under your department leads.

Loaders & Pushers

Truck loading/unloading, case pushes, dock organization, and gear moves to keep your load-in and load-out on schedule.

Backline Assistants

Support for instrument setup, stage changes, mic stands, stands, and cable management for touring or local acts.

Lighting & Audio Helpers

Non-skilled helpers for hanging, running, and striking under your lead technicians and house crew.

On-Site Lead Hands

Experienced crew leads to interface with your production manager, direct the crew, and keep the day flowing smoothly.

Multi-Day Builds & Festivals

Consistent teams for multi-day builds, show runs, and strikes so you’re not breaking in a new crew every day.
